Our Recruitment Process

  1. Role and expectation alignment with HR / TA
  2. Targeted sourcing within the BPO talent pool
  3. English proficiency and behavioral screening
  4. Availability, commitment, and shift readiness validation
  5. Structured candidate submission with screening notes
  6. Tracking time-to-fill and early attrition metrics to optimize hiring quality

Talent Acquisition Metrics We Focus On

  • Time-to-Fill: We prioritize rapid sourcing to reduce vacancy durations.
  • Quality of Hire: Screening tailored to call center KPIs and language fluency.
  • Early Attrition Rate: Minimizing drop-offs during probation through expectation alignment.
  • Retention Support: Replacement candidates provided when needed under agreed terms.

Commercial Model

Apex Recruitment Group operates on a success-based pay-per-hire model for English call center roles.

  • Fees apply only after successful onboarding
  • Defined probation completion period
  • Replacement support within agreed timelines
